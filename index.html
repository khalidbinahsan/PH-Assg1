Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending DevConf 2026.

What does my conference pass include?

Your pass includes access to the selected conference days, keynote sessions, expo areas, and the standard networking activities listed in each tier.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes. Ticket transfers are available before the event begins. Please contact the registration team with the original attendee information and the new attendee details.

Are workshops included in every plan?

Standard passes include a limited workshop selection, while Pro includes unlimited workshop access. Team packages follow the Pro experience for the included seats.

Will session recordings be available after the event?

Yes. Standard passes include 30 days of recordings, while Pro gives lifetime access to recordings and event content.

Is there a refund policy?

Refunds are available within the published refund window before the event. After that date, tickets may still be transferable depending on availability and policy rules.

Do you offer group or company discounts?

Yes. The Team package is designed for groups and includes seats, lounge access, and support tailored for organizations attending together.
